ABOUT US

We make this website to help travelers find information about museums in Pahang, Malaysia. The website provides detailed information about the various museums located in Pahang, including their history, exhibits, and visiting hours. It also includes information about the history, culture, and art of Pahang, which will give visitors a deeper understanding and appreciation of the state.
