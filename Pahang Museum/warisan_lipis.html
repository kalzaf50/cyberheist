WARISAN LIPIS MUSEUM

Kuala Lipis


Warisan Lipis Museum in Pahang, Malaysia is a cultural museum that showcases the rich history and heritage of the Lipis district in the state. Housed in an old building, the museum features a variety of exhibits, artifacts, and multimedia displays that provide an in-depth look at the district's development over the years. From the colonial era to the present day, the museum provides a glimpse into the cultural, economic, and social evolution of the Lipis district.

Visitors can explore the different eras of Lipis' history through interactive exhibits, hands-on displays, and multimedia presentations. The museum also features several themed galleries, such as the traditional costume gallery, where visitors can learn about the different types of traditional clothing worn in the district and their cultural significance. Additionally, the museum has a section dedicated to the district's natural resources and the industries that have developed around them, such as agriculture, mining, and tourism. Overall, Warisan Lipis Museum is an excellent destination for anyone interested in learning about the history and cultural heritage of the Lipis district in Pahang, Malaysia.
