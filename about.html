OVERVIEW

YOUR CHARACTER

Meet Cipher, the enigmatic protagonist in this high-stakes cyber saga. A master of shadows and secrets, Cipher, an ex-spy, emerges from the clandestine world of Company A's rule over New York City. Armed with a sharp mind and an arsenal of cutting-edge gadgets, Cipher is not just a player – but the city's last hope.

ABOUT

Embark on an electrifying mission as an undercover agent thrust into the heart of New York City, now under the tight grip of the formidable Company A. Your task: a daring recapture operation. Immerse yourself in the pulse-pounding world of espionage, where you'll navigate the sprawling urban landscape, infiltrate enemy lines, and ultimately break into the underground lair housing the elusive main server. The fate of New York rests in your hands—hack, infiltrate, and escape before it's too late!
