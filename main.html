ABOUT US

ABOUT US

We named our website "Pahang Museo" because the word Museo means "a place where people can go to view a collection of art, artifacts, or scientific specimens." This name accurately represents the goal of the website as a comprehensive guide to museums in Pahang, Malaysia.

We make this website to help travelers find information about museums in Pahang, Malaysia. The website provides detailed information about the various museums located in Pahang, including their history, exhibits, and visiting hours. It also includes information about the history, culture, and art of Pahang, which will give visitors a deeper understanding and appreciation of the state.

OBJECTIVES

  • Help travelers find information about museums in Pahang, Malaysia.
  • Give visitors a deeper understanding and appreciation of the state.
  • To promote the heritage and culture of Pahang to travelers.
